Online Mode
Control via internet from anywhere

Use your mobile or any browser to control appliances through MQTT protocol. Works from anywhere with internet.

👉 Control from office, travel, anywhere

Offline Mode
Works without internet using AP hotspot

ESP32 creates its own WiFi network. Connect and control via browser at 192.168.4.1 — no internet needed.

👉 Works even during internet outages
📺
IR Remote Mode
Use any standard TV/AC remote

Physical button press on any assigned IR remote instantly toggles your appliances. Simple, fast, elderly-friendly.

👉 No phone needed — just press a button
🚶
PIR Auto Mode
Motion-based automatic control

PIR sensor detects room occupancy. Lights auto-turn ON on entry and switch OFF after 10 minutes of no movement.

👉 Saves electricity automatically

Safety Instructions

Kripya in instructions ko zaroor padhe — safety first

🚨
Critical Safety Rules
Ye rules kabhi ignore na karein
⚡
Installation ke time Power OFF rakhein Main circuit breaker ya MCB band kar dein. Kabhi bhi live wire ke saath kaam na karein. Electric shock fatal ho sakta hai.
🔧
Wiring ke liye Electrician ki Help lein Relay module wiring kisi qualified electrician se karwayein, especially agar aapko electrical work ka experience nahi hai.
💧
Device ko Water / Moisture se Door Rakhein ESP32 aur relay module water-resistant nahi hain. Bathroom, kitchen ke paas ya bahar use na karein. Nami se short circuit ho sakta hai.
⚠️
General Cautions
  • ⚠️
    Overloading se bachein — relay max load check karein
  • ⚠️
    Device ko direct sunlight mein na rakhein
  • ⚠️
    Bachon ki pahunch se door rakhein
Prevention is better than cure
✅
Best Practices
  • ✅
    WiFi password regularly change karte rahein
  • ✅
    ESP32 firmware regularly update karein
  • ✅
    Relay connections ek baar monthly check karein
Safe system = happy home 🏠
